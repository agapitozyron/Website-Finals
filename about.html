Who We Are

Crafting Spaces
Since 2010

At Santi Blinds, we believe window coverings are more than a functional necessity — they are the architects of atmosphere. Every product we craft is a careful study in how light shapes a room, a mood, a life.

Founded on the principle that refined living begins at the window, we bring precision manufacturing, considered design, and enduring materials to every home and space we touch. What started as a small workshop in Valenzuela City has grown into Metro Manila's most trusted window covering brand.

Every consultation begins with listening. We take time to understand your space, your light preferences, and the way you live — before recommending a single product.

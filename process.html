How It Works

From Measure

A seamless four-step journey — designed entirely around your space and your schedule.

01

Consultation

We begin every project with a free in-home consultation. One of our experienced design consultants will visit your space, listen to your vision, and walk you through our complete collection of blinds, fabrics, and finishes.

This isn't a sales pitch — it's a conversation. We want to understand how light moves through your rooms, what privacy matters to you, and what aesthetic fits your home.

  • Free, no-obligation home visit
  • Full product samples brought to your door
  • Expert light and privacy advice
  • Available weekdays & weekends
Free Consultation
02
Precision Measuring

Measure & Design

After the consultation, we take precise measurements of every window — down to the millimetre. At the same time, you finalise your fabric, finish, colour, and operating mechanism choices with the help of our design team.

We create a detailed specification for every window in your project, ensuring that the final product fits perfectly and looks exactly as you imagined.

  • Millimetre-precise window measurements
  • Fabric & finish selection
  • Operating mechanism advice
  • Written quote provided
03

Custom Fabrication

Your blinds are crafted to exact specifications in our production facility. We use premium-grade materials sourced from trusted suppliers, and every product passes a quality inspection before it leaves our workshop.

Standard turnaround is 7–10 working days. Rush production is available on request for urgent projects.

  • In-house production facility
  • Premium-grade materials only
  • Full quality inspection pre-dispatch
  • 7–10 working day standard turnaround
Custom Fabrication
04
✓
Expert Installation

Installation

Our trained installation team arrives at a time that suits you. We install every blind with care, precision, and respect for your home — walls are protected, surfaces cleaned, and every blind is tested before we leave.

We also walk you through operating your new blinds, adjust any settings, and leave your space spotless. Our work isn't done until you're completely satisfied.

  • Flexible scheduling to suit you
  • Clean, careful installation
  • Full operating walkthrough
  • Satisfaction guaranteed
